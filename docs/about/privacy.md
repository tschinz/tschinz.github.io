# Privacy Policy

![](img/privacy.svg){.center width="40%"}

**Last updated:** January 2026

Your privacy matters. This page explains how we handle your data when you visit Zentauri.

## :shield: Our Commitment

We believe in **privacy-first analytics**. This website is designed to respect your privacy while still allowing us to understand how the site is used and how we can improve it.

## :bar_chart: Analytics

We use [Tianji](https://tianji.dev/) for website analytics. Tianji is a privacy-focused, open-source analytics platform.

### What makes Tianji different?

!!! tip "GDPR & CCPA Compliant"
    Tianji is fully compliant with:

    - **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) - European Union
    - **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act) - United States

    This means your privacy rights are protected by design, not just by policy.

!!! success "No Cookies"
    Tianji **does not use cookies** to track visitors. This means:

    - No annoying cookie consent banners needed
    - No tracking across websites
    - No personal data stored in your browser

!!! info "What we collect"
    Tianji collects only anonymous, aggregated data:

    | Data                  | Purpose                             |
    | --------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
    | Page views            | Understand which content is popular |
    | Referrer              | Know how visitors find us           |
    | Country (approximate) | Understand our audience geography   |
    | Device type           | Optimize for desktop/mobile         |
    | Browser               | Ensure compatibility                |

!!! warning "What we DON'T collect"
    - :x: Personal information (name, email, etc.)
    - :x: IP addresses (not stored)
    - :x: Cookies or tracking identifiers
    - :x: Cross-site browsing history
    - :x: Fingerprinting data
